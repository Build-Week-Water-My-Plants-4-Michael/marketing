Plant Parenthood

Plant Parenthood is a free app that reminds you to water your houseplants. If you always forget to water flowers at home or office this is the right app for you.

How It Works

Choose

Pick a plant in your home

Capture

Capture flower’s avatar with your camera

Set

Set watering interval – 6 days, for example. Simple as that!

Features

Track

Track all plants in your home

Notifications

Receive notification when a plant needs water

Configure

Configure notifications (reminders) time - in the morning, at noon or in the evening

Support

Support for watering intervals from half-day to twenty days

Avatars

Create flower avatars using phone’s camera

Experience

Special design for unique user experience
